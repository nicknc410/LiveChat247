Teen Social Skills 101: A Guide to Surviving the Most Awkward Time of Your Life  - Book

$9.95

Are you struggling with low self-esteem? Through this empowering self-help book, teenagers will have access to their very own social skills’ survival toolkit from a voice that is both caring and motivating. Author Daniela Lozano gets upfront and personal to walk you through the modern-day challenges of adolescence and their effective solutions. “If confidence is the key to conquering teen life, then social skills are the key to becoming self-confident.” In a one-of-a-kind educational guide, Lozano shares her most valuable tips and advice to successfully navigate your most awkward teen moments. Following an-depth exploration of issues ranging from peer pressure, to heartbreak, to body image—teens will learn how to reach their full potential through the author’s real-life experience.
